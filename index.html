Case Studies

Driving +150% Lead Growth for a Multi-Location Service Business

The Challenge: A successful multi-location pet services business had a strong brand but was nearly invisible in local search results. Their website traffic was stagnant, and more importantly, they were not generating leads (phone calls, appointment bookings) through Google, especially from the crucial "map pack."

My Strategy: I developed and executed a comprehensive Local SEO strategy focused on three core pillars:

  • Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization: I performed a full optimization of all location profiles, ensuring complete and accurate information, strategic category selection, and consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) data.
  • On-Page Local SEO: I created and optimized unique, geo-targeted landing pages for each location, incorporating local keywords, service details, and schema markup to signal relevance to search engines.
  • Local Link Building & Citations: I built a foundation of high-quality local citations and pursued link-building opportunities with local community sites and relevant industry directories to boost local authority.

The Impact:

  • +150% Increase in Clicks-to-Call from Google Business Profiles within 6 months.
  • 85% Growth in organic website traffic originating from local search queries.
  • Achieved consistent **Top 3 Local Pack rankings** for high-value keywords across all major service locations.

Scaling Content Quality with a Data-Driven Editorial System

The Challenge: A high-volume digital publisher was struggling with inconsistent content quality and poor SEO performance. Articles were often misaligned with user search intent, leading to low engagement, and the revision process was inefficient, creating bottlenecks that throttled growth.

My Strategy: Instead of fixing articles one by one, I designed and implemented a centralized, data-driven content brief system to serve as the single source of truth for all content creation. The system integrated in-depth keyword research, user intent analysis, and a structured on-page SEO checklist into every brief. I then trained the writers and editors on how to leverage this system, making SEO a core part of the creative process.

The Impact:

  • Primary driver behind a +50% increase in organic engagement across multiple brands.
  • Achieved 100% on-page SEO consistency across hundreds of new articles.
  • Reduced content revision cycles by 40%, significantly improving publishing velocity.
