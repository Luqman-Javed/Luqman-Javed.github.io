Case Studies & Impact

Case Study: Driving +150% Lead Growth for a Multi-Location Business

A multi-location veterinary clinic with a strong offline reputation had a weak online presence, making them invisible in local search results for high-intent keywords.

The Challenge

The business suffered from unoptimized Google Business Profiles, inconsistent NAP data across the web, a lack of localized website content, and poor review management.

The Strategy

  • Executed a complete overhaul of Google Business Profiles for all locations.
  • Developed unique, SEO-optimized location pages on the main website.
  • Conducted a comprehensive citation audit to fix inconsistencies and built new, high-authority local citations.
  • Implemented a proactive reputation management system to generate and respond to reviews.

The Impact

  • +150% Increase in Clicks-to-Call from Google Business Profile
  • 85% Growth in Website Traffic from Local Search
  • Achieved Top 3 Local Pack Rankings for 15+ high-value keywords
  • Improved Average Rating from 3.8 to 4.7 stars on Google

Case Study: Scaling Content Production with a Data-Driven Brief System

A digital publisher struggled with inconsistent content quality, poor on-page SEO across articles, and long, inefficient revision cycles with freelance writers.

The Challenge

Content was being produced without a clear, data-informed strategy. This led to articles that failed to rank, required multiple rounds of edits, and did not align with business goals.

The Strategy

  • Developed and implemented a standardized, data-driven content brief system.
  • Each brief integrated in-depth keyword research, user intent analysis, competitor insights, and a clear on-page SEO checklist (headings, internal links, etc.).
  • Trained writers and editors on how to use the new system to streamline production and ensure every piece of content was created with purpose.

The Impact

  • -40% Reduction in revision requests and editing time
  • 100% On-Page SEO consistency across 200+ new articles
  • Contributed to a +50% increase in overall organic engagement
  • Empowered writers to produce higher-quality content, improving team morale and efficiency.
